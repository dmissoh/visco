Risk Assessment

Understand your results

Visceral fat surrounds your internal organs. Unlike subcutaneous fat, high levels are linked to serious health risks.

Healthy

Your visceral fat is within a healthy range. Maintain your current lifestyle with regular exercise and balanced nutrition.

Elevated

Your levels are above optimal. Consider increasing physical activity and reviewing your diet. Track regularly to monitor changes.

High Risk

Elevated visceral fat is associated with increased health risks. Consult a healthcare professional for personalized guidance.
